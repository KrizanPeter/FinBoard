1) Registration

Registration is quite straightforward. Just fill in your name which will be displayed as your username, email and password

2) Initial setup - Setting base data

Base data represent settings which are strongly bound to your account

Date of first snapshot - date of the first evidence of your portfolio. If you have no your own personal history just leave it predefined with date of registration.

Periodicity of snapshots - Define how often you have done the record or how often you will need to do it.

2) Initial setup - Define resources

Define resources Create your resources which you have been tracking or want to track in future.

2) Initial setup - Create resource groups

Create resource groups Allows you to group your resources.

    Example: You want to create an Overall property group which will include all your resources:
  • Fill the name of the resource group into the "Create resource group" tab and hit "Add resource group."
  • Select specific group from dropdown in "Add resource to group" tab
  • Check specific resources (in this case all of them) that u want to add to the group and hit the "Add to group" button.

The current status of your groups should be visible under the "List of your resource groups" tab.

2) Initial setup - Fill data (snapshots)

Fill data (snapshots) Allows you to fill the historical data of your resources and consists of two parts:

  • Data checker - generated snapshots list based on your base data from step 1. with information about the status of the snapshot.
  • Create aggregate snapshot - a form that will create snapshots for a specific date. U can also edit your values with values specific to date in the Date checker

After filling in all data Data checker should be marked as correct with a green checkmark and the button "To Dashboard" should be enabled.

3) Customize your dashboard

Customize your dashboard Allows you to create different types of graph overviews. There are three types of graphs:

  • Resource / Line chart - will generate line chart of single resource.
  • Resource group / Line chart - will generate a line chart of resource groups where the amount is the cummulative value of specific resources.

4) Keep your finboard updated

Updating finboard Based on your periodicity you will need to update your data for this u can use the "Add snapshot" page

In case that new snapshot should be created, the system automatically indicates that your data are data valid anymore and some of them are missing. Find the missing snapshot date and fill it.

After adding a new snapshot all graphs will be updated automatically
