1) Registration

Registration is quite straightforward. Just fill your name which will be displayed as your user name, email and password

2) Initial setup - Setting base data

Base data represent settings which are strongly binded to your account

Date of first snapshot - date of first evidence of your portfolio. If you have no your own personal history just leve it pre-defined with date of registration.

Periodicity of snapshots - Define how often you have been doing your evidence or how often you will have to do it.

2) Initial setup - Define resources

Define resources Create your own resources which you have been tracking or you want to track in future

2) Initial setup - Create resource groups

Create resource groups Allowes you to group your resources.

    Example: You want to create Overal property group which will include all your resources:
  • Fill the name of resource group into "Create resource group" tab and hit "Add resource group."
  • Select specific group from dropdown in "Add resource to group" tab
  • Check specific resources (in this case all of them) which u want to add into group and hit "Add to group" button.

Current status of your groups should be visible under "List of your resource groups" tab.

2) Initial setup - Fill data (snapshots)

Fill data (snapshots) Allowes you to fill historical data of your resources and consists of two parts

  • Data checker - generated snapshot list based on your base data from step 1. with information about status of snapshot.
  • Create aggregated snapshot - furmulary which will create snapshot for specific date. U can also edit your walues with click on specific date in Date chcecker

After filling all data Data checker should be marked as correct with green checkmark and button "To Dashboard" should be enabled.

3) Customize your dashboard

Customize your dashboard Allowes you to create different types of graph overviews. There are three types of graphs:

  • Resource / Line chart - will generate line chart of single resource.
  • Resource group / Line chart - will generate line chart of resource groups where amount is cummulative value of specific resources.

4) Keep your finboard updated

Updating finboard Based on your periodicity you will need to update your data for this u can use "Add snapshot" page

In case new snapshot should be created system automaticaly indicate that your datas are not valid anymore and some of them are missing. Find the missing snapshot date and fill it.

After adding new snapshot all graphs will be updated automatically
