What is Finboard?

Finboard was designed to help understand and visualize own portfolio based on own preferences and needs. Do you need to group specific resources, visualize them and watch them grow? Finboard will do it for you!

What Finboard offer

Finboard allows you to specify resources that you want to track. It also brings an opportunity to group them into resource groups. All that u need to set up resource/resource groups every month (or your designated period) and fill the balance.s

Please remember...

Finboard is in a very early phase of development as a hobby project and as an, every application may contain bugs. Regardless it offers quite interesting functionalities. So enjoy!
